learnpnp::Marking::Marking ( const std::map< std::string, int > &  inmap)

Initializes the marking from a map.

The map must have the id of the state as the key and the number of tokens in the state as the value. The states are identified by their position in the map. Recall that iterators of std::map traverse the structure in the order imposed by the keys.

Imposes a total order upon markings.

a < b iff a has less states than b or they have the same number of states and there exists a state s such that all the states that precedes s in the internal ordering of the markings are less then or equal to their corresponding state in b and s is strictly less then it corresponding state in b.

Parameters:
ais a marking
bis a marking
Returns:
true iff a precedes b in the order described.
