bool learnpnp::operator< ( const Marking &  a,
const Marking &  b 
)

Imposes a total order upon markings.

a < b iff a has less states than b or they have the same number of states and there exists a state s such that all the states that precedes s in the internal ordering of the markings are less then or equal to their corresponding state in b and s is strictly less then it corresponding state in b.

Parameters:
ais a marking
bis a marking
Returns:
true iff a precedes b in the order described.
